WAVE is the leading provider of en-route wireless power charging solutions that overcome traditional battery limitations in electric vehicles by delivering wireless power using electrical infrastructure embedded in the roadway and vehicle-mounted receiver plates.

WAVE's Wireless Power Transfer (WPT) is created through magnetic fields that move power from underneath the roadway to the vehicle, without wires or cables, and is the most powerful and efficient single pad wireless charging system deployed by a U.S. company for public transit.

Candidates must be eligible to work in the US.
