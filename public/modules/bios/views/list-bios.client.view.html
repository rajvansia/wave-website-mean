Executive

WAVE’s Executive Team is comprised of engineers, technology experts and business professionals dedicated to bringing to market the latest innovations in wireless power technology for vehicle applications.

Power Electronics Engineering

The Power Electronics Engineering Team designs and builds WAVE’s wireless power systems, including all testing, troubleshooting, and integration. The team also works on research and development projects to expand the scope of WAVE’s technology.

Software Engineering

The Software Engineering Team designs, develops, and validates WAVE’s advanced charging control algorithms in a real-time embedded environment.

Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical Engineering Team creates and implements WAVE’s mechanical system design and validation specifications, as well as assisting with technology implementation.

Manufacturing Engineering

The Manufacturing Engineering Team oversees all project management, including project schedule, installation, and customer service.
